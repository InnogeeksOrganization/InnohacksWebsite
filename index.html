About Innohacks

INNOHACKS is an annual hackathon organized by Innogeeks at the KIET Group of Institutions, Delhi-NCR, Ghaziabad. It aims at bringing talented young minds together to celebrate their technical passion and build exciting projects! This time we are organising InnoHacks in hybrid mode(both online & offline). The hackathon consists of three phases:

  • Phase 1: 20th March - 8th April 2023 (Online Ideation Phase)
  • Phase 2: 8th April - 10th April 2023 (Shortlisting of top 30 teams)
  • Phase 3: 22th April - 23th April 2023 (24 hours offline Hackathon)

Frequently asked questions

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! We believe that you’re stronger as a team than apart. You can form teams of 2-4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and development skills.

How much will it cost?

There will be no registration fee. Registration will only be done through Unstop portal.

What can I build?

Possibly something that resonates with the theme of the event. It can be a website, software, an applet, or maybe even a piece of hardware. We would love to see anything that solves a social problem or goes with our agenda.

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

What do I have to bring?

Bring your laptop, Chargers, Cables, Something Cool to show-off, pair of cloths, handtowels, toothbrush, extension board, etc. Anything that violates our Code of Conduct will lead to disqualification.

What if I have more queries?

Feel free to reach us out on our Discord Server or drop us a mail at innohacks@kiet.edu.
